Group School Project:

We were given a group project and in this project we had to create a system that automated daily operations

We decided to do the Hospital Management System where a local hospital was used as our scenario and we meant to automate most of the daily operations.

What we auutomated in the hospital management system

  • The reception queues will be reduced because now patients were allowed to make appointments online

  • Check the availability of the doctor and try fit in to your schedule

  • No backlog because patients medical history is stored on cloud and can be retrieved any time and easier to share it with other doctors if a patient wants to switch hospitals for personal reasons

  • Doctors prescription to the patient can be send to them via email or be sent to the dispensary in that hospital
